WING EATING CONTEST

  Every Sunday, challengers are invited to take part in the famed 50-wing challenge.You’ll have one hour to eat 50 of the Game Day Grills delectable BBQ wings in the hopes of earning a plaque to put on the Wall of Fame.If you’re able to finish the challenge you’ll receive a t-shirt, $15 gift certificate, and, of course, bragging rights as the wing champion.

OPEN MIC NIGHT

  Shift the spotlight from the game to the stage! Join us for Open Mic Night every Wednesday,a vibrant blend of sports and entertainment. Whether you're a seasoned performer or a first-timer, showcase your talent in stand-up comedy, live music, and more. Enjoy great food,and cheer on local talent. It's a night of unforgettable performances and free drinks for all performers.
